## 📌 Project Overview 
### A comprehensive business intelligence dashboard analyzing **62,884 sales transactions** across 8 countries, 67 stores, 11 brands and 5 currencies from 2016–2021. **Built to answer:** - Where is revenue growing and declining? - Which products and categories drive the most profit? - Who are our most valuable customers? - How efficient are our stores vs online channel? - What is our customer retention rate?

## 🔑 Key Findings 
### **Revenue peaked in 2019 at $18M** — declined 49% in 2020 (COVID impact) 
### **Cameras & Camcorders** = highest profit margin at 60.1% 
### **Senior Citizens (65+)** = highest revenue customer segment at $26M 
### **Online channel** generates $11M with just 1 virtual store — most efficient 
### **96% customer churn** over 5 years — critical retention problem identified 
### **US dominates** with 54% of total revenue across 24 stores 

## 🎯 Strategic Recommendations
### 1. **Invest in digital channel** — online AOV is significantly higher than physical 
### 2. **Target senior demographic** — highest revenue segment, currently underserved 
### 3. **Fix customer retention** — loyalty program could recover $3–5M annually 

## 🛠️ Technical Highlights 
### **Data Model:** Star schema — 1 fact table + 4 dimension tables 
### **DAX Measures:** 20+ measures including YTD, MoM%, YoY%, Rolling 3M 
### **Power Query:** Currency normalization via daily exchange rate merge 
### **Advanced Features:** What-If parameter, drill-through, custom tooltips 
### **AI Visuals:** Key Influencers, Decomposition Tree 
### **Forecasting:** Revenue forecast with 95% confidence interval 
### **Cohort Analysis:** Customer retention matrix 2016–2021
